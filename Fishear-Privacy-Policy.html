Fishear Privacy Policy

Messages

Fishear is an application for Android mobile phones that allows users to send and receive messages via their mobile carrier (using the SMS protocol). Messages are only ever transported between mobile phones and mobile carriers. Fishear does not collect and does not store these messages on third-party servers. Also, Fishear never shares these messages with third parties.

Personal Data

Fishear does not collect and does not share any personally identifiable information.

Device permissions

Fishear requests certain permissions that allow it to access the User's device Data as described below.

Please note that the revoking of such permissions might impact the proper functioning of this Application.

  • Contacts permission

    Used for accessing contacts and profiles on the User's device.

  • SMS permission

    Used for accessing features related to the User's messaging including the sending and receiving of SMS.
